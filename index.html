How to Play Blackjack

Welcome to Blackjack. The objective of the game is to get as close to 21 as you can without going over.

  • Number cards are worth based on the cards value.
  • Kings, Queens, and Jacks are worth 10.
  • Aces are worth 1 or 11, depends on if the cards exceed 21 based on calculation.
  • The player and the dealer begin with 2 cards.
  • Click "Hit" if you wish to draw another card.
  • Click "Stand" if you wish to finish your turn, to which the dealer begin their round.
  • The dealer will play until their satisfied with their postion.
  • The closest person to the 21th score will win.
