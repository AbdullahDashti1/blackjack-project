How to Play Blackjack

Welcome to Blackjack. The objective of the game is to get as close to 21 as you can without going over.

  • 1- Number cards are worth based on the cards value.
  • 2- Kings, Queens, and Jacks are worth 10.
  • 3- Aces are worth 1 or 11, depending on if the total exceeds 21.
  • 4- The player and the dealer begin with 2 cards.
  • 5- Click "Hit" to draw another card.
  • 6- Click "Stand" to finish your turn and let the dealer play.
  • 7- The dealer will play until they reach at least 17.
  • 8- The closest score to 21 wins.
